Fightmare

  • Genre: Beat'em Up
  • Mode: Singleplayer
  • Development Time: 3 weeks
  • Team: 9 people
  • Platform: PC

Fightmare is born from a school project to mix two games. We took the gameplay from Street Fighter and the ambiance from Little Nightmares to get this dark and distressing Beat'em Up.

Characters' attacks

  • Players can make the character attack using various inputs.
  • Each attack is split between Startup, Active and Recovery frames, calculated with frames in 60fps. Active frames are a part where the character cannot move and the hitbox for damage is active.
  • The character has a specific set of attacks. First, a simple attack that can be chained 3 times for a combo. There is also a special attack, slower but deadlier. Going forward while the special can make a forward special which deplete character's health but does a lot of damage.
  • Using twice the forward input makes a blitz attack. It was supposed to launch enemies in the air, but it was cut during the projet. Finally, the character can grab enemies to block and hit them.

Enemies' attack

  • Enemies spawn and attack the player one at the time.
  • Most of them are basic and only hit the character. But there is also Grappler enemies who can grab her, letting the others hit more easily.

Scoring and timer

  • Each successful attack of the character increases the score. Losing life will decreases it.
  • A timer runs during the level and makes the player lose if it reaches 0.

Collectables

  • A weapon can be picked up to deal more damage, but breaks after a few hits.
  • Enemies have to chance to drop a healing item. When the character walks on it, health is partially restored.
