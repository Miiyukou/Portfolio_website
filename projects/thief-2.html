Thief 2: The Metal Age

  • Objective: New feature integration
  • Development Time: 1 week
  • Team: 2 people
  • Platform: PC

As a school project, we had to think about a feature that could be in Thief 2: The Metal Age. With the help of a Level Designer, we made a level with boxes that could hide one body, or Garrett.

Contents

  • Hide in box
  • Hide corpses
  • Skills worked on
  • Team members

Hide in box

  • The new feature is an interactable level element that can be used to hide.
  • By interacting with it, the character goes inside. It helps avoid being detected.
  • If the character was already spotted before entering the box, guards will come get them anyway.

Hide corpses

  • In Thief 2, it is possible to knock down guards. This feature has been recreated in this project too.
  • When a guard is unconscious, they can be transported in a hide box, where the body is well hidden.
  • To remind the player that a body is in the box and they can't enter it anymore, the box is now closed.
