NieR: Automata

  • Objective: Feature recreation
  • Development Time: 2 days
  • Type: Personal Project
  • Platform: PC

For 2 days, I decided to train my understanding of coding with the recreation of an existing feature: the pod from NieR: Automata.

Contents

  • Pod shoot
  • Pod program
  • Lock enemies
  • Skills worked on

Pod shoot

  • As in the original game, the pod can shoot to enemies and destroy some of their projectiles.
  • Visually thhe pod move downwards when it starts shooting, and get back to its original position when its done.

Pod program

  • In NieR: Automata, the pod can get different "super attacks" with cooldown. I chose to make the first one. knock down guards. This feature has been recreated in this project too.
  • The pod shoots a laser in front of it or towards a locked enemy. To add a feeling of power, time slows when the laser shoots.

Lock enemies

  • Although it's not directly a feature from the pod, I decided to do the enemy locking. It is very useful and helps the pod shoots on specific enemies.
  • When locked, the enemy stays at the center of the camera and the player can move around.
